About Us

We are a team of engineering students from Gonzaga University competing in the Baja SAE competition. Each year, we design, build, and race an off-road vehicle to tackle the toughest terrain and push the limits of performance, teamwork, and innovation.

Our Vehicle

Engine: Briggs & Stratton 10hp
Weight: 420 lbs
Top Speed: 35 mph
Frame: Chromoly Steel
